	\section*{More maths}
	Fraction: $\frac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{dy}{dx}$ \newline
	Sum: $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} = \displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}$ \newline
	Limit: $\lim_{n \to \infty} = \displaystyle\lim_{n \to \infty}$ \newline
	Integral: $\int_a^b = \displaystyle\int_a^b$
